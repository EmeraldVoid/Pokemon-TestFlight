# Meet the Gym Leaders

In this post, I'm excited to share a visual spoiler for the gym leaders in **Pokémon TestFlight**. I know they may not be the most polished or well-designed, but I hope you'll bear with me. As someone who isn't a skilled spriter, I decided to improvise and upgrade a few trainers to Gym Leaders when I couldn't find any online that I liked. At a later date, I'll be sharing more information on **Stratus** & **Coalton**, including their teams, so stay tuned for that!

I'm still in the process of creating the badges for each Gym Leader, so the links you see below are just placeholders for now. I'll be sure to update them when the badges are ready!
