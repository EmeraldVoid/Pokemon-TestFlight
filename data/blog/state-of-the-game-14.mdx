I just wanted to go over the Gym Leaders and their respective badges for TestFlight. This will be a short post.

### Gym Leaders & Badges

- **Stratus**: Flying type Pokémon, Tornado Badge
- **Coalton**: Rock type Pokémon, Stalagmite Badge
- **Kai**: Water type Pokémon, Shell Badge
- **Bryor**: Grass type Pokémon, Petal Badge
- **Hanzo**: Poison type Pokémon, Venom Badge
- **Tricia**: Ice type Pokémon, Icicle Badge
- **Reginald**: Steel type Pokémon, Spike Badge
- **Taiga**: Fire type Pokémon, Solar Badge

I'll be posting more information on their team compositions later, so stay tuned for that!
