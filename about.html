Welcome to the Kalmera region!

Step into the world of Pokémon in Kalmera, a fresh region full of new challenges and classic adventures. Explore diverse environments, build your team, and take on Kalmera’s Gym Leaders and Elite Four in your journey to become Champion. With plenty to discover and even more to conquer in the endgame, Kalmera offers a streamlined, battle-focused experience for trainers who just want to play.


Open World

Kalmera is designed with freedom in mind—once you receive your starter, the region is yours to explore. Whether you head north into the snowy peaks or dive south into sunlit shores, there’s no set path or linear progression. Choose your own adventure and take on the challenges in any order.


Battle Kalmera's Gym Leaders

Each Gym Leader in Kalmera offers unique strategies and battle conditions, ensuring every match is a challenge worth remembering. You’ll face off against a wide variety of teams and tactics, making every victory feel earned and every badge a milestone.


Catch Pokémon

Kalmera is home to a curated selection of Pokémon from across generations, along with a few regional surprises. With carefully placed habitats and balanced encounter rates, catching and training your ideal team feels rewarding and accessible from the start.
